# Apple Inc. Financial Performance Analysis (2020-2024)

## 1. Problem & User
This project analyzes Apple Inc.'s financial performance and capital structure from 2020 to 2024, to evaluate its profitability, operational efficiency, and financial health for academic ACC102 course assessment.

## 2. Data
- **Data Source**: Wharton Research Data Services (WRDS)
- **Access Date**: [April 24,2026]
- **Key Fields**: Revenue, Net Income, ROE, ROA, Gross/Operating/Net Profit Margin, Operating Cash Flow, Debt-to-Asset Ratio, Capital Structure

## 3. Methods
- Data extraction and cleaning from WRDS
- Financial ratio calculation (profitability, leverage, growth metrics)
- Data visualization with Python (Matplotlib) to show trend analysis
- Comparative analysis of 5-year financial performance

## 4. Key Findings
- Apple maintained strong and stable profitability across 2020-2024, with ROE consistently outperforming ROA, driven by moderate financial leverage.
- Gross, operating and net profit margins remained at high levels, reflecting effective cost control and operational efficiency.
- Revenue and net income achieved steady growth, supported by robust operating cash flow, though revenue growth moderated after 2022.
- The debt-to-asset ratio stayed stable at around 80-86%, indicating a mature, low-risk capital structure, with debt accounting for 84.4% of total assets in 2024.
